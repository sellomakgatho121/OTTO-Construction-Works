What We Do

Our Services

From fire protection to finishing touches — we deliver quality workmanship across every trade.

01

Passive Fire Protection

We specialise in passive fire protection systems designed to contain fires within compartments, protect escape routes, and ensure your building meets all fire safety regulations. Our services include fire-stopping, cavity barriers, fire-rated walls and ceilings, and structural steel protection.

Every installation is carried out in accordance with SANS 10400 and approved building standards, giving you peace of mind that your property is protected.

Why choose us

  • SANS 10400 compliant installations
  • Fire-stopping and compartmentation
  • Structural steel fire protection
  • Fire-rated walls, floors, and ceilings
  • Cavity barriers and smoke seals
02

Tiling

From sleek bathroom renovations to durable commercial flooring, our tiling service delivers precision and quality. We work with ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, and vinyl tiles to create beautiful, long-lasting surfaces.

Whether it's a feature wall, kitchen backsplash, or full floor installation, we ensure level substrates, consistent spacing, and professional finishing every time.

Applications

  • Floor and wall tiling
  • Bathroom and kitchen renovations
  • Commercial flooring
  • Feature walls and backsplashes
  • Outdoor paving and cladding
03

Plaster Work

Our plastering team delivers smooth, durable finishes for both internal and external surfaces. We handle all types of plaster work including traditional sand and cement plaster, gypsum plaster, and decorative finishes.

From new builds to renovations, we ensure your walls are straight, smooth, and ready for painting or finishing. We also offer screeding for level floor preparation.

Our Work

  • Internal wall plastering
  • External rendering
  • Floor screeding
  • Decorative plaster finishes
  • Patch repairs and renovations
04

Bricklaying

Strong foundations start with quality bricklaying. Our experienced bricklayers handle everything from foundation work and cavity walls to extensions and garden walls. We work with brick, block, and stone to create structurally sound, visually appealing masonry.

Every course is level, every joint is consistent, and every structure is built to last. Whether it's a new building or a wall extension, we deliver precision and strength.

Capabilities

  • Foundation and blockwork
  • Cavity wall construction
  • House extensions
  • Garden and retaining walls
  • Stone and brick masonry
05

Cleaning Service

A clean site is a safe site. Our cleaning service covers post-construction cleanup, commercial cleaning, and general deep cleaning. We remove debris, dust, and waste, leaving your property spotless and ready for use.

We also offer ongoing cleaning contracts for commercial properties, ensuring your premises remain clean and presentable at all times.

Services

  • Post-construction cleaning
  • Commercial cleaning
  • Deep cleaning
  • Debris and waste removal
  • Ongoing maintenance contracts
06

Electrical Service

Our electrical team handles all types of electrical work — from new installations and wiring to repairs, maintenance, and upgrades. We ensure every job is completed to code, safely and efficiently.

Whether you need a full rewire, additional outlets, lighting installation, or fault finding, our qualified electricians deliver reliable, professional service.

What We Do

  • Electrical installations
  • Wiring and rewiring
  • Lighting and power points
  • Fault finding and repairs
  • Electrical maintenance
