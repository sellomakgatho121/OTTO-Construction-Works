About OTTO

A registered South African construction company built on honesty, integrity, and exceptional service.

Our Story

Building Excellence,
Delivering Trust

OTTO Construction Works was founded by Lee Roy Jacobs and Stacey Lee Jacobs with a clear mission: to provide professional, reliable construction services that South Africans can trust.

Registered under the Companies Act (2026/287740/07), OTTO Construction Works brings together years of hands-on experience in the construction industry. We understand that every project, big or small, represents an investment for our clients — and we treat it with the care and professionalism it deserves.

Our name, OTTO, stands for our commitment to Outstanding workmanship, Trustworthy service, Timely delivery, and Open communication. These are not just words — they are the principles that guide every project we undertake.
